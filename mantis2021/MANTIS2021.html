Due to the evolving nature of the CoViD-19 pandemic, speakers will give their talks virtually.  Participants may join either in person, or virtually.  The Zoom link will be emailed to all registered participants.

It is expected that all in-person participants will be able to provide proof of vaccination against CoViD-19.  As per University of Michigan guidelines, masks will be required indoors, and all guests must complete the ResponsiBLUE Screening Check each day they are on campus.
